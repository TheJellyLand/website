Details

The smart contract powering the creation of these NFTs can be seen . At the base is an ERC721 OpenZepplin NFT smart contract which is extended to control certain properties such as the price and supply of each Jelly minted.

The first 100 minted NFTs of this collection will be at no cost (except the gas fee to mint). After this first batch has been minted, subsequent NFTs will be of varying price based on the overall supply already minted. The figure shows the predicted price of the NFT, with the final one costing 0.1 ETH.

Price = tokenSupply * 0.00001 ETH
The total number of Jelly NFTs to be minted will be 10000, but only a limited number will be minted in each hue of purple. There will also be a collectors edition released in the future for Unique Jellys who appear throughout the comic series. Also some surprises to be on the lookout for!

About

Jellyman was a cartoon character created by an 8th grader from New York back in 2007. It was a bit of a small town hit with family and friends. Online games, animations, and merchandise were created around various Jelly characters. In many ways it was still the early days of the modern internet back then and this was quite a unique experience. Life progressed for the creator but Jellyman was left behind. Now in 2023 with the advent of NFTs, a new JellyLand is back with many of the same characters and more! If you are interested in more of the story check out the blog post here. As we get ramped up again and building on a modern technology stack check out the Roadmap.
